MazeOnCube

Description: User navigates maze on cube faces and seeks target
Programmer: Matthew
Compiler: Smart Mobile Studio 2.2

Introduction

Matthew began learning Smart Pascal by gradually changing one of our starter programs, and this is the first program of his own starting from scratch. He had a good, ambitious idea for a beginner and made excellent use of our page entitled Exclusion Zones. Matthew showed the determination and resilience essential for an effective programmer. To play the game, use the wasd keys to navigate through the six faces of the cube in search of the target blue square that has been randomly placed on one of them. When you touch it your score increments and the target appears in another random location. Playing this game is a good memory exercise; you need to remember the faces you have visited in the current search.

If the program does not work after clicking on the display, try another browser such as Chrome.

Technical Features

The program benefits from:

  • own procedures;
  • use of inbuilt routines such as Randomize, RandomInt, FillRectF, FillTextFLineToF, MoveToF, GetImageData and BeginPath;
  • appropriate use of case statements;
  • handling of keyboard input;
  • enumerated type.
