RetroKeeper3D

Description: Keeper tries to save shots directed at corners of goal
Programmer: Matthew
Compiler: Smart Mobile Studio 2.2

Introduction

Matthew continues to show imagination and flair with his second published Smart Pascal game. To play the game use the space bar (or s key if the space gives an unwanted side effect) to receive a well-directed shot to one of the corners of the goal. Attempt to save the shot by pressing one of the keys a, d, j or l to aim the keeper's stick at the top left, top right, bottom left or bottom right corner, respectively. A blue flag waves after each goal and a red flag after each save - a nice touch!

Technical Features

The program benefits from:

  • thorough commenting;
  • own procedures;
  • use of inbuilt routines such as Randomize, RandomInt, FillRectF, FillTextFLineToF, MoveToF and BeginPath;
  • appropriate use of case statements;
  • handling of keyboard input;
  • set constants;
  • for loops;
  • display of bold, italic text;
  • enumerated type.
